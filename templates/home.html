Welcome to UChicago Junior Achievement

Junior Achievement at the University of Chicago (UChicago JA) is a chapter of non-profit organization Junior Achievement USA (JA USA). We help connect UChicago students with opportunities to volunteer through JA USA. JA programs focus on teaching students about financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and career readiness and impact 4.4 million students annually.
